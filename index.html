SWRC Fit

SWRC Fit can fit several soil hydraulic models to measured soil water retention data. Copy your soil water retention data in the textbox below and press "Calculate" button. Before you use your original data you can see how it works by selecting a sample data from the pulldown menu. Read more instruction.
